0: Finely cut ginger rhizomes (50 g) were placed in a round-bottomed flask and 130 mL of distilled water was added.
1: The hydrodistillation process was performed 3 h long in a Deryng apparatus.
2: After this duration, the obtained oil was mixed with n-hexane, dried from water over silica gel, and stored in a sealed vial at a low temperature (4 C) before the GC-MS analysis.
