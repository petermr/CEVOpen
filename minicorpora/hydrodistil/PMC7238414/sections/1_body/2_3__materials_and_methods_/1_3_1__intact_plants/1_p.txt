0: The primary explants of  E. maritimum and  E. alpinum were collected from Adam Mickiewicz Botanical Garden in Poznan, Poland in September 2017.
1: The voucher specimens have been deposited in the Department of Pharmaceutical Botany and Plant Biotechnology of Poznan University of Medical Sciences under the numbers: H-M-2017-101 ( E. maritimum) and H-AP-2017-102 ( E. alpinum).
2: The permission for the collection of the organs from the protected species in order to use them for scientific research was granted by the Regional Director for Environmental Protection in Poznan.
