0: One-way ANOVA analysis was performed using SPSS version 26.0 to compare egg number, egg hatch rate, larval duration, and adult longevity followed by Tukeys test for mean separation.
1: Statistical significance was established as  P < 0.05.
2: All other comparisons between treatments were analyzed using students t-test at 5% level.
3: The LC 10, LC 50 and LC 90, LT 10, LT 50, and LT 90, as well as their respective 95% confidence intervals, were calculated by probit analysis (Polo Plus software).
